Grandma's Secert Apple Pie

This apple pie recipe has been in family for generations and never fails to deilight.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-size apples, peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375° F (190°C.)
  2. In a bowl, mix the sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Toss the apples, in the sugar- cinnamon mixture.
